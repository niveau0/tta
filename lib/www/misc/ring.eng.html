During the third age, long before the ring-war began, the world was still in uproar. Sauron the maiar, servant of the banned evil Morgoth, and his dark minions always seeked for more might. The ringwraith, highest of Saurons followers, fought wars against human and elves.

In TTA you play a person living during the year 2400 t.a. The One Ring (of which you see the inscription above) and all the other high rings were already forged and Sauron lost his ruling ring. But nobody found it yet! Or not? Find out by yourself...
