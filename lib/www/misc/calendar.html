Here you can see an overview of the year, how the month were named and where the special days are positioned.

The year has 365 days, but each month consists of 30. The five special days lay 'outside' of the months. There are

  • 1. Yule, last day of the year
  • 2. Yule, new years day
  • 1. Lithe, last day of the first half
  • 2. Lithe, first day of the second half
  • Midyear's day, like the name says, the middle of the year
