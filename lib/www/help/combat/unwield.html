The Third Age - Help unwield


   Type:        command
   Usage:       unwield <item>
                where <item> is the name of the tools you want to stop 
                wielding.
                
   Description: Remove some tool from the position it needs for usage. E.g.
                carry your  sword in a less  straining way than  holding it
                ready for battle. But your need to re-wield it for fight.
   

 See also: 'wield'
