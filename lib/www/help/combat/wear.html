The Third Age - Help wear


   Type:        command
   Usage:       wear <item> [on <location>] 
                where <item> is the name of the clothes you want to wear
                and <location> specifies where you want to wear the item.
                
   Description: Put on something you are holding.  Armor, rings, whatever.
                This could take some time.

   Example:     wear hat on third head
   

 See also: 'remove'
