The Third Age - Help wield


   Type:        command
   Usage:       wield <item>
                where <item> is the name of the tools you want to wield.
                
   Description: Put some tool into a proper position for usage. E.g. take
                your sword at its grip, so you are ready for battle.
   

 See also: 'unwield'
