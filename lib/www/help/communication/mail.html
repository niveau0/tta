The Third Age - Help mail


   In certain  places in the world  you may a  find post - office or  other    
   institutions willing to accept your mail and  deliver it to the intended
   receiver.  

   The mailtext editor understands the following commands:

   ~?      displays the following help:

   **      Finish text.
   ~q      Cancel text.
   ~l      Show complete text.
   ~L      Show complete text with line numbers.
   ~a x    Append after line x.
   ~i x    Insert before line x.
   ~A      Append at end of text.
   ~I      Insert before top of text.
   ~d x    Delete Line x.
   ~d x y  Delete Lines from x to y.
