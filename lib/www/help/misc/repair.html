The Third Age - Help repair


   Type:        command
   Usage:       repair <item>
                
   Description: You fix a jagged blade, put a new string on your bow and 
		just make your weapon as good as new. You should have
		knowledge about the material of the weapon you want to
		repair. A hammer is recommended if you want to work on metal. 
                
   Example:     repair dagger
   

See also: 'metal craft', 'wood craft', 'skills'
