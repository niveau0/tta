The Third Age - Help bind


   Type:        command
   Usage:       bind <living> with <rope>
                
   Description: Binding means, you lay a rope in as many loops as possible
		in a pattern, as complicated as possible, around a victim.
		Lowering his limbs abilities to move until it can't perform
		any physical action anymore.

   Example:     bind alyn with chain
   

 See also: 'skills', 'rope mastery'
