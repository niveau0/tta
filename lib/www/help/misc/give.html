The Third Age - Help give


   Type:        command
   Usage:       give <item> to <someone|something>
                
   Description: If you want to transfer some of your items to someone you 
                offer them just by giving them to someone or somthing.
                The receiver needs to answer with the 'accept' command.
                
   Example:     give beer to tall elf
                    

 See also: 'accept'
