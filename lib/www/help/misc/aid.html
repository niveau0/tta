The Third Age - Help aid


   Type:        command
   Usage:       aid [broken] <body part> of <name>
                
   Description: Help someone suffering from bloodloss or bandage the broken
		bones. Your success to stop the bleeding depends on your first
		aid skill, the bandage of a broken limb on your second aid
		skill.
                
   Example:     aid left arm of findulas
		aid broken leg of dergios
