The Third Age - Help alias


   Type:        command
   Usage:       alias <abbrev> <command>
	        alias -l
	        alias -d <abbrev>
                
   Description: (Re)define or remove an abbreviation from your alias list.
                To show all your aliases use 'alias -l'.
		Use the '$'-sign to execute your previous command again.

   Example:     alias ga get all    - Make 'ga' an abbrev. for 'get all'
    	    	alias ga get apple  - Redefine 'ga', now its 'get apple'   
    	    	alias -d ga         - Remove the abbreviation 'ga'
		$                   - do the previous command again
