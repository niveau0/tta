The Third Age - Help cook


   Type:        command
   Usage:       cook <recipe name> with <cooking container>
                
   Description: Lets you prepare food or a potion according to
                the prescription given as the <recipe name>. You 
                need a <cooking container> and the appropriated 
                ingredients to succeed. This command is most usefull
                in conjuction with the skill "cookery".
                
                
   Example:     cook dragonsoup with iron pot
                cook potion of healing with brass cauldron
   

 See also: 'skills','cookery'
