The Third Age - Help carve


   Type:        command
   Usage:       carve <object> from wood
                
   Description: Lets you carve an object out of wood. You need a piece
                of wood large enough to get the object out of it and
                you also need a fletching set. This command is most
                usefull in conjunction with the skills "fletching" and
                "wood craft". 
                
   Example:     carve arrow from wood
   

 See also: 'skills', 'fletching','wood craft'
