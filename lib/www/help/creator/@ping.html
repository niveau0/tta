The Third Age - Help @ping


   Type:        command
   Usage:       @ping <ipname>
                
   Description: Send 'ping' to a specified ipname. Check '@status' idle-
		times to see if your ping was successful.

   Example:     @ping tta
   

 See also: '@status', '@open', '@close', '@join', '@msg', '@list', '@tell',
	   '@who'
