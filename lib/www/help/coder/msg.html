The Third Age - Help msg


 
   Type:     	command   
   Usage:       msg
                
   Description: The command 'msg' is used by the Immortals to toggle entering 
		and leaving player messages.  If you want to keep abreast of 
		who is logging in and out, then 'msg' is the command for you.
 
   Example:     > msg
		Entering and leaving players are now shown.
		> [Seclan - enters from 134.106.23.6]
		> [Cadderly - logged out]

		> msg
		Entering and leaving players are no longer shown.
