The Third Age - Help idle


 
   Type:        command
   Usage:       idle
                	
   Description: The command 'idle' gives you a list of all the players
		currently logged into the mud and their respective times 
		of their idleness.
		It is seperated by hour--minute--second.
 
   Example:     > idle
		Idle:
		manwe_________0h___1m___3s
		erikose_______0h___0m___0s
