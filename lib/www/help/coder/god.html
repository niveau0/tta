The Third Age - Help god


 
   Type:        command
   Usage:       god <text>
		@ <text>
                	
   Description: 'God' and '@' are what the Immortals use to speak to each 
		other.  This text is ONLY seen by other Immortals, however, 
		keep in mind that it is seen by ALL Immortals.
 
   Example:     > god TTA ROCKS!
		@ Erikose: TTA ROCKS!
