The Third Age - Help force


 
   Type:        command
   Usage:       force <playername> <command>
                
   Description: The command 'force' is used by Immortals to force players
		to do the <command> argument.  
		** NOTE ** NO GOD SHOULD EVER FORCE A PLAYER TO DO ANYTHING 
		WITHOUT ASKING FIRST AND RECEIVING PERMISSION, with the 
		exception of your tester.  ABUSE OF THIS COMMAND WILL MOST
		CERTAINLY LAND YOU IN HOT WATER WITH THE ADMINISTRATION OF TTA.
 
   Example:     > force erikosejr smile
		Erikosejr smiles happily.
