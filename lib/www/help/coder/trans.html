The Third Age - Help trans


 
   Type:        command
   Usage:       trans <player>
                
   Description: The command 'trans' is used to transfer a player from the
		room that they are in to the room where you are. 
 
   Example:     > trans erikosejr
   		Erikosejr arrives in a puff of smoke.


 See also: 'goto'
