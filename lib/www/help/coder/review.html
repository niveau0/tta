The Third Age - Help review


 
   Type:        command
   Usage:       review
                
   Description: The command 'review' is used to review your movement messages.
 
   Example:     
	
    > review
    teleport out: looks into your ear and struggles his way into your head!
    teleport in : suddenly struggles and appears from out of your ear!  OW!
    walk msg    : slowly waddles his way
    run msg     : zoomy zoom zooms
   

 See also: 'set_walk_msg', 'set_run_msg', 'set_teleport_in', 
	   'set_teleport_out'
