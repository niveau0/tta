The Third Age - Help climate


   The climate in the world of Middle-Earth in the third age differed
   from region to region. For 'The Third Age', we adopted the climate 
   chart made by Karen Wynn Fonstad in her 'The Atlas of Middle-Earth'
   mapped on the different regions taken from 'Middle Earth - The Wizards'
   by ICE. Please keep up to these restrictions when you choose a climate
