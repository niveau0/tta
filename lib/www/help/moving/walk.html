The Third Age - Help walk


   Type:        command
   Usage:       walk <direction>
	  	walk <item|person>

   Description: Start to walk into a specified direction, you will keep on
                walking until you are to tired, something blocks your way 
                or you just 'stop'. 
		You may also walk to someone/something.
                
   Example:     walk west
		walk 1n2e
		walk finduilas
   

 See also: 'run'
